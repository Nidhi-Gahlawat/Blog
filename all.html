So you landed here to read about the books I read till now. Well the list is not too long but still worths an overview. I have always been a reader because my father used to brought so many books when I was a kid. I got addicted to books when I was around 13 years in class 8th as I got the access to library. I used to borrow books mostly from school library and a couple of times from a classmate who used to borrow them from her neighbour and was an ardent novel addict. Then after 10th I almost left reading books other than academic ones due to pressure to score good in boards. After 12th I continued reading and explored the various genres.
